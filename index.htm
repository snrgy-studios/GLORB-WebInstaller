Welcome to the GLORB Web Installer!

  1. Select the firmware and any specific options you want to install (probably default).
  2. Hit Install. A list with available COM ports will apear.
  3. Plug in your GLORB to a USB-C port on your computer.
    Connect to the port that appears in the list.
  4. Press Install GLORB and wait for the installation to finish.
  5. Set your WiFi credentials to connect the GLORB to your network.
  6. Plug in your GLORB to a dedicated power supply and control it through the app!
