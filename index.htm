Welcome to the GLORB Web Installer!

  1. Plug in your GLORB to a USB-C port on your computer. Firmware 0.1.0-b1 will be installed.
  2. Choose Plain or Bluetooth version.
  3. Hit "Install" and select the correct COM port. No device found?
  4. Wait for installation to finish and connect your GLORB!
