Welcome to the GLORB Web Installer!

  1. Plug in your GLORB to a USB-C port on your computer.
  2. Choose firmware release. Select version, microphone and ledmap.
  3. Hit "Install" and select the correct COM port.
    (If unsure, plug it in again to see which one appears in the list.)
  4. Press install and wait for the installation to finish!
